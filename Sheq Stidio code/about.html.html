About Sheq Studio

Built on clarity, trust, and thoughtful design.

Sheq Studio exists to help care-led brands communicate with confidence, credibility, and intention — without noise or gimmicks.

Founder & Creative Director

Dr. Arshdeep Kaur

Dr. Arshdeep Kaur is a dentist turned brand strategist and founder, specializing in building high-trust, minimal, and modern brand identities for care, wellness, and lifestyle businesses.

Her clinical background gives her a deep understanding of trust, communication, and user behaviour — which she now blends with design strategy to create brands that feel clear, credible, and visually elevated.

At Sheq Studio, she leads creative direction, brand strategy, and digital experience development — transforming complex ideas into refined, intentional visual systems. Her approach is rooted in clarity, psychology, and thoughtful design.

Focus areas

Brand identity, digital experience, healthcare communication

Design philosophy

Minimal. Intentional. Psychology-led.

What defines the work

Clarity over clutter. Trust over trends.
